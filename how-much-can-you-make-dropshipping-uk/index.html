What is dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a business model where you sell products without holding inventory. Instead, you partner with a supplier who ships the products directly to your customers. This allows you to focus on marketing and sales, while the supplier takes care of order fulfillment.

Dropshipping has become increasingly popular in recent years, as it allows entrepreneurs to start an online business with low startup costs and minimal risk.

The history and myth of dropshipping in the UK

Dropshipping has been around for many years, but it has only become popular in the UK in recent years. The rise of ecommerce and online marketplaces has made it easier than ever to start a dropshipping business.

There are many myths surrounding dropshipping, such as the idea that it is a get-rich-quick scheme. In reality, dropshipping requires hard work and dedication to be successful.
