Top 20 Franchise Business in the Philippines

Before we dive into the list, let's discuss what franchise business is and why it is a smart investment. A franchise is a type of business model where an established company grants the right to use its brand name, products, services, and processes to another person or company in exchange for an initial fee and ongoing royalties.

Franchise business is a smart investment because it offers a proven business model, a recognized brand, training and support, and a higher success rate compared to starting a business from scratch. By investing in a franchise, you can avoid the common pitfalls of starting a business and increase your chances of success.
