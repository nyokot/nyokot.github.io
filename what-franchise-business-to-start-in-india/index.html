What is a franchise business?

A franchise business is a business model where the franchisor licenses its trademark, products, and business model to a franchisee. The franchisee pays an initial fee and ongoing royalties to the franchisor, and in return, the franchisor provides support and training to the franchisee.

My personal experience with a franchise business was with a fast-food chain. As a franchisee, I had access to their proven business model, marketing support, and purchasing power. This made it easier to start and run the business successfully.
