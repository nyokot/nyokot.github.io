What is Amazon Dropshipping?

Amazon dropshipping is a business model in which sellers list products for sale on Amazon without actually holding inventory. When an item sells, the seller purchases the item from a third-party supplier and has it shipped directly to the customer. This allows the seller to avoid the costs and risks associated with holding inventory.
