Explanation of What Are the Best Franchises to Own

Franchising is a business model where a franchisor grants a license to a franchisee to use their brand name, products, and services in exchange for an initial fee and ongoing royalties. The franchisee operates their own business using the franchisor's proven system and support. The best franchises to own are those that have a strong brand, a proven system, and a profitable business model.
