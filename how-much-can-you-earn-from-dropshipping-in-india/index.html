What is dropshipping in India?

Dropshipping is a business model where a retailer does not hold inventory of the products sold. Instead, the retailer buys products from a supplier who ships the products directly to the customer. This allows the retailer to focus on marketing and sales, without worrying about inventory management and fulfillment.

Question and Answer

Q: Is dropshipping legal in India?

A: Yes, dropshipping is a legal business model in India.

Q: How much money do I need to start dropshipping in India?

A: You can start dropshipping in India with as little as INR 10,000. However, it is important to set aside a budget for marketing and advertising.

Q: How long does it take to make money with dropshipping in India?

A: The time it takes to make money with dropshipping in India depends on several factors such as niche, product pricing, marketing strategy, and competition. It may take a few months to a year to see significant results.

Q: Do I need to register a business to start dropshipping in India?

A: It is not mandatory to register a business to start dropshipping in India. However, it is recommended to register as a sole proprietorship or private limited company for legal and tax purposes.
