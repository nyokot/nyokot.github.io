What is a Service Mast?

A service mast is a metal pole that connects the electrical service line from the utility company to your home's electrical panel. It is a crucial component of your home's electrical system and should be replaced if it is old, damaged, or worn out.

How to Replace a Service Mast

Replacing a service mast is a complex project that should only be handled by a licensed electrician. It involves disconnecting the electrical service line, removing the old mast, installing the new mast, rewiring, and upgrading the electrical system if necessary.

What If You Don't Replace Your Service Mast?

If you don't replace your service mast, it can lead to electrical hazards, such as electrical fires and electrocution. It is crucial to replace your service mast if it is old, damaged, or worn out to ensure the safety of your home and family.
