What is Uber in South Africa?

Uber is a ride-sharing company that connects passengers with drivers through a mobile app. The company launched in South Africa in 2013 and has since expanded to several major cities, including Johannesburg, Cape Town, and Durban.

The history and myth of Uber in South Africa

Uber's entry into South Africa was met with some resistance from taxi associations, who argued that the company was operating illegally and taking business away from local drivers. However, Uber managed to overcome these challenges and establish a foothold in the country.
