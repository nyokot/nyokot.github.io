To start an ecommerce business for free, you need to follow a few essential steps. Firstly, identify your niche and target audience. What is Ecommerce Business?

Ecommerce business refers to the buying and selling of goods and services online. It encompasses various activities such as online retail, dropshipping, and digital products sales.

History and Myth of Ecommerce Business

The concept of ecommerce business dates back to the 1960s when organizations began exchanging business documents electronically. However, it was not until the 1990s that ecommerce gained popularity with the advent of the internet. Today, ecommerce is a thriving industry, with global retail ecommerce sales expected to reach $4.9 trillion by 2021.
