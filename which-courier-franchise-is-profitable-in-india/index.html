What is Blue Dart?

Blue Dart is a courier and logistics service provider in India, founded in 1983. They are known for their reliable and fast delivery services, both domestically and internationally. They have a strong brand presence and have won numerous awards for their excellence in the industry.

History and Myth of Blue Dart

Blue Dart was founded by Tushar Jani, Clyde Cooper, and Khushroo Dubash in 1983. They started with just one aircraft and a handful of employees, but quickly expanded to become one of the leading courier and logistics companies in India. There are no known myths associated with Blue Dart, as they have always maintained a strong reputation in the industry.
