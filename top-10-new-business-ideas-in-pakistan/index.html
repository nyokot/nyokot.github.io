E-commerce

E-commerce has seen a significant surge in Pakistan over the last few years, with a growing number of online marketplaces and retailers launching across the country. The rise of e-commerce in Pakistan has been driven by a number of factors, including an increasing number of internet users, a growing middle class, and a shift towards digital payments.

For entrepreneurs looking to start an e-commerce business in Pakistan, there are many opportunities available, including online retail, marketplace platforms, and digital payments solutions. However, the market is highly competitive, and success requires careful planning, a deep understanding of the local market, and a focus on customer experience and satisfaction.
