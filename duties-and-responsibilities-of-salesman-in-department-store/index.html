Salesmen in department stores often face challenges that can make their job difficult. From dealing with difficult customers to meeting sales targets, the job can be demanding. However, with the right skills and knowledge, it can also be rewarding.

So, what are the duties and responsibilities of a salesman in a department store? Their primary responsibility is to assist customers in finding and purchasing products. They must be knowledgeable about the products they are selling and be able to provide customers with information about them. Salesmen must also be able to work as part of a team, maintain a clean and organized work environment, and meet sales targets set by their employer.

In summary, the duties and responsibilities of a salesman in a department store include assisting customers in finding and purchasing products, being knowledgeable about the products they are selling, working as part of a team, maintaining a clean work environment, and meeting sales targets.
