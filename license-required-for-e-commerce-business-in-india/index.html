Question and Answer about License Required for E-commerce Business in India

Q: What is GST registration, and why is it required for e-commerce businesses in India?

A: GST registration is required for e-commerce businesses with an annual turnover of more than Rs. 20 lakhs. It is a tax registration that enables businesses to collect and pay taxes on behalf of the government.

Q: What is FSSAI license, and why is it required for e-commerce businesses selling food products?

A: FSSAI license is required for e-commerce businesses selling food products online. It ensures that the food products sold by the business are safe and comply with the food safety standards set by the government.

Q: What is trademark registration, and why is it important for e-commerce businesses?

A: Trademark registration is important for e-commerce businesses to protect their brand and intellectual property. It ensures that other businesses cannot use their name or logo without permission, and it gives the business legal protection in case of infringement.
