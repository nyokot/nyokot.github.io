Question and Answer about Starting a Food Business from Home in Malaysia

Q: What kind of permits and licenses do I need to start a food business from home in Malaysia?

A: You will need to obtain a business license, a food handling permit, and a halal certification if you plan on selling halal food products.

Q: Can I sell my food products online?

A: Yes, you can sell your food products online through social media platforms or e-commerce websites. However, you will still need to follow legal requirements and regulations.

Q: How can I market my food products?

A: You can market your food products through social media, word of mouth, and by participating in local food festivals and events.

Q: Is it necessary to attend food safety courses?

A: Yes, attending food safety courses and obtaining certifications is crucial to ensuring that your products meet food safety standards.
