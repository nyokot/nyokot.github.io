What is an Internet Service Provider Franchise?

An internet service provider franchise is a business model where a franchisor provides the brand name, business model, and support to franchisees who operate their own local internet service provider businesses. Franchisees pay a fee to the franchisor for the right to use their brand name and business model, and in turn, receive training, marketing support, and ongoing assistance from the franchisor.

The History and Myth of Internet Service Provider Franchises

The first internet service provider franchise was established in the early 2000s, and since then, the industry has grown significantly. While there are many myths surrounding franchise ownership, such as the idea that franchisees have no control over their business, the reality is that franchisees have a great deal of autonomy while still benefitting from the support and resources provided by the franchisor.
