What Is Dropshipping in the UK?

Dropshipping is a business model in which the seller does not hold any inventory. Instead, the seller lists products for sale on their website or e-commerce platform, and when a customer places an order, the seller forwards the order to a third-party supplier who ships the product directly to the customer. The seller makes a profit by charging a higher price for the product than they pay to the supplier.
