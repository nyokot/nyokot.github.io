The top 10 fast food franchises include McDonald's, Subway, KFC, Burger King, Pizza Hut, Dunkin' Donuts, Taco Bell, Wendy's, Domino's Pizza, and Starbucks. These franchises have become household names and are known for their consistent quality and service.

History and Myth of the Top 10 Fast Food Franchises

Many of the top 10 fast food franchises have interesting histories. For example, McDonald's was founded in 1940 by Richard and Maurice McDonald. The brothers originally owned a hot dog stand but eventually transitioned to burgers. Another interesting fact is that the original name for KFC was Kentucky Fried Chicken, but it was changed in 1991 to reflect a move away from fried food.

One hidden secret of the top 10 fast food franchises is that many of them have secret menus. For example, at McDonald's, you can order a "McGangBang," which is a combination of a McChicken and a McDouble. At Burger King, you can order a "Suicide Burger," which is four beef patties, four slices of cheese, bacon, and special sauce.
