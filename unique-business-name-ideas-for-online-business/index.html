Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a business name is already taken?

A: You can search for business names on the USPTO website or do a quick Google search to see if a name is already in use.

Q: Can I trademark my business name?

A: Yes, you can trademark your business name to protect it from being used by others.

Q: Should I choose a name that's easy to spell?

A: Yes, choosing a name that's easy to spell can help customers find your business online and can make it easier for them to recommend your business to others.

Q: Can I change my business name later on?

A: Yes, you can change your business name later on, but it can be costly and time-consuming. It's best to choose a name that you're happy with from the start.
