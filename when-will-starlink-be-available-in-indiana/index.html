What is Starlink?

Starlink is a satellite internet service provided by SpaceX, the company founded by Elon Musk. The service aims to provide high-speed internet to areas that are not served by traditional internet service providers. Starlink uses a network of satellites that orbit the earth, providing internet service to users on the ground.
