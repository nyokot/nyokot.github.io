What is a salon franchise under 5 lakhs in India?

A salon franchise is a business model where a franchisor grants a franchisee the right to use their brand, products, and services in exchange for a fee. The franchisee operates the business according to the franchisor's guidelines and standards.

A salon franchise under 5 lakhs in India is a franchise that has an initial investment of less than 5 lakhs. These franchises are affordable and offer a low-risk opportunity for aspiring entrepreneurs to start their business.
