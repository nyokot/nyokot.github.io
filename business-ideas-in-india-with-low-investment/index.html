Business Ideas in India with Low Investment

One of the best business ideas in India with low investment is to start a food business. You can start a small food stall or a food truck and sell delicious food items. You can also start a catering business and provide food for events and parties. Another excellent option is to start a home-based food delivery service.

Another low investment business idea is to start a cleaning service. You can provide cleaning services for homes, offices, and other commercial spaces. All you need is some cleaning equipment and supplies, and you are good to go.

You can also start a home-based tutoring service. You can provide tutoring services for students in your local area. You can also offer online tutoring services and reach a broader audience.

If you are creative, you can start a home-based handicraft business. You can create unique handicraft items and sell them online or at local markets. You can also offer custom-made handicraft items for customers.
