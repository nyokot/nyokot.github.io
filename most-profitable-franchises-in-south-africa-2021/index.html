What is Most Profitable Franchises in South Africa 2021?

A franchise is a business model where an entrepreneur purchases the right to use a company's name, trademark, and business model. In return, the franchisee pays a fee to the franchisor and agrees to follow the franchisor's rules and guidelines. The franchisor provides training, support, and marketing materials to help the franchisee succeed.
