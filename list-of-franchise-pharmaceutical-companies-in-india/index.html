What is Franchise Pharmaceutical Companies in India?

A franchise pharmaceutical company is a business model in which a pharmaceutical company grants a license to an individual or group to operate a store or outlet using the company's brand, trademark, and business model. The franchisee pays a fee to the franchisor for the right to use the brand and receives support in areas such as marketing, training, and operations. Franchise pharmaceutical companies can be a successful way to enter the pharmaceutical industry, as they provide a proven business model and established brand.
