One of the best online business ideas from home in Marathi is affiliate marketing.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is a type of performance-based marketing in which a business rewards one or more affiliates for each visitor or customer brought about by the affiliate's own marketing efforts. This is a great way to start a business as you don't need to create your own products.

You can start by finding a product or service that you are passionate about. Once you find a product, you can promote it through your website, social media, and other channels. When someone clicks on your affiliate link and buys that product, you earn a commission.
