What is Can You Make Money with a Cleaning Franchise?

A cleaning franchise is a business model in which an entrepreneur purchases the rights to operate a cleaning business under an established brand name. The franchisor provides support, training, and marketing materials to the franchisee.

The franchisee operates the business according to the franchisor's guidelines, pays royalties, and receives ongoing support from the franchisor. The franchisee also benefits from the established brand name, which can help to attract customers and build trust.
