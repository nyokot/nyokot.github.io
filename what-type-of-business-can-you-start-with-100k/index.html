What is a Franchise?

A franchise is a business model in which an individual (the franchisee) pays a fee to use the name, branding, and business model of an established company (the franchisor). Franchise businesses often have a higher success rate than independent startups because they benefit from the established brand recognition and systems of the parent company.

The History and Myth of Starting a Franchise with 100k

Many people believe that starting a franchise requires a large investment, but this is not always the case. While some franchises require a significant upfront investment, others can be started with as little as 100k. It is important to do your research and choose a franchise that aligns with your interests and budget.
