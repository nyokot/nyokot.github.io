What Is a Kumon Franchise and How Does It Work?

Kumon is a tutoring franchise that specializes in math and reading programs for children of all ages. The franchise model works by providing franchisees with a proven business model and support system to set up and operate their own Kumon centers. Franchisees are responsible for securing a location, hiring staff, and marketing the center to attract students.
