Many factors can influence the profitability of a franchise, such as location, competition, and the overall market demand. It's essential to have a clear understanding of the financial potential before making any investment.

According to Kumon, the average annual gross income for a Kumon Math and Reading Center franchise is $150,000. However, this figure can vary depending on factors such as the location and the number of students enrolled. The initial investment for a Kumon franchise can range from $69,000 to $149,000, including the franchise fee and initial startup costs.

What Is a Kumon Franchise and How Does It Work?

Kumon is a tutoring franchise that specializes in math and reading programs for children of all ages. The franchise model works by providing franchisees with a proven business model and support system to set up and operate their own Kumon centers. Franchisees are responsible for securing a location, hiring staff, and marketing the center to attract students.
