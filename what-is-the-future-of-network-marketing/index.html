Q: What is network marketing?

A: Network marketing is a business model in which independent distributors sell products or services on behalf of a company, and earn commissions on their sales as well as the sales of their team members.

Q: Is network marketing a pyramid scheme?

A: No, network marketing is not a pyramid scheme. Pyramid schemes are illegal and involve recruiting people into a scheme where the only way to make money is by recruiting more people.
