What is Franchise Business in Chennai with Low Investment?

Franchise business is a model where a person (franchisee) buys the right to use an existing business model and brand of a company (franchisor). The franchisee pays an initial fee and ongoing royalties to the franchisor for the right to use their brand, products, and services. Franchise business in Chennai with low investment is a franchise business model that requires a low initial investment.

Franchise business in Chennai with low investment can be found in various sectors such as food and beverage, retail, education, and services. The franchisor provides the franchisee with training, support, and marketing materials to help them succeed.

The concept of franchising dates back to the 1850s when Isaac Singer, the founder of Singer Sewing Machines, started selling franchises to expand his business. Since then, the franchise business model has grown in popularity and can be found in various industries.
