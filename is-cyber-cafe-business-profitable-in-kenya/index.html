What is Cyber Cafe Business?

A cyber cafe is a business that provides internet access to customers for a fee. In Kenya, cyber cafes are popular among students, job seekers, and people who don't have access to the internet at home. Cyber cafes also offer printing, scanning, and photocopying services.

History and Myth of Cyber Cafe Business in Kenya

Cyber cafes became popular in Kenya in the early 2000s when the internet was still a luxury. They provided a convenient way for people to access the internet without having to buy expensive computers and pay for internet connectivity. However, with the increasing availability of smartphones and affordable data plans, the demand for cyber cafes has declined.

The Hidden Secret of Cyber Cafe Business Profitability in Kenya

The hidden secret to running a profitable cyber cafe business in Kenya is to offer unique services and target a specific market segment. For example, you could offer gaming services, printing and scanning services, or target students and job seekers who need internet access for research and job applications.
