The first step in registering your small business in Cape Town is to decide on the legal structure of your business. This will determine what type of registration you need to apply for. The most common types of legal structures for small businesses in South Africa are sole proprietorship, partnership, private company, and non-profit organization. Once you have decided on the legal structure, you will need to register your business with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC).

In summary, to register a small business in Cape Town, you need to:
- Decide on the legal structure of your business
- Register your business with the CIPC
- Obtain any necessary licenses and permits
- Register for tax
- Open a business bank account
