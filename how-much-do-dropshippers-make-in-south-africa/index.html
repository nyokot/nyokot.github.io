What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a business model where you sell products without keeping inventory. Instead, you partner with a supplier who ships the products directly to the customer. As a dropshipper, you act as a middleman between the supplier and the customer.

One of the main advantages of dropshipping is that you don't have to invest in inventory upfront. You only pay for the products that you sell. This means that you can start a business with very little capital.
