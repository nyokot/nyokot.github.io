What is Food Franchise under 50 Lakhs in India?

A food franchise under 50 lakhs in India is a type of business where a franchisor grants the right to use its brand name, products, and services to a franchisee for a fee. The franchisee is then responsible for running the business and adhering to the franchisor's standards and guidelines.

The franchise fee typically includes training, marketing support, and ongoing operational support from the franchisor. This makes it an attractive option for those who want to start their own business but don't have prior experience or knowledge in the food industry.
