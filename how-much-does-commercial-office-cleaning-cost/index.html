The cost of commercial office cleaning services can vary depending on several factors, such as the size of your office, the cleaning frequency, and the services required. On average, commercial office cleaning services cost between $0.07 and $0.15 per square foot. For example, a 5,000 square foot office can cost between $350 and $750 per month for daily cleaning.
