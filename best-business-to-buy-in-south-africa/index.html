Understanding Franchise Business

A franchise business is a model in which a franchisor grants the right to use their brand, products, and services to a franchisee for a fee. The franchisee receives training, marketing, and ongoing support from the franchisor, while the franchisor benefits from the franchisee's local knowledge and investment. Franchise businesses offer a lower risk profile than starting a business from scratch and can be an excellent opportunity for those new to entrepreneurship.
