What is an Italian Ice Business?

An Italian ice business involves creating and selling delicious frozen treats made from fresh fruit, sugar, and water. Italian ice is similar to sorbet but has a smoother texture and is typically served in a cup or cone. It's a popular dessert choice in the summer months and can be found in a variety of flavors, from classic lemon to more exotic options like mango and passionfruit.

The History and Myth of Italian Ice

Italian ice has a long and fascinating history, dating back centuries to ancient Rome. The first recorded recipe for a frozen fruit dessert was created by Emperor Nero, who is said to have sent slaves to the mountains to collect snow and then mixed it with honey and fruit juice to create a refreshing treat. Over time, this recipe evolved into what we now know as Italian ice.
