What is a Courier Business?

A courier business is a company that provides delivery services for packages and parcels. These services can include same-day or next-day delivery, international shipping, and specialized services for fragile or valuable items. Courier businesses can operate locally, regionally, or nationally, and can serve both individuals and businesses.

History and Myth

Courier services have been around for centuries, dating back to ancient civilizations that used messengers to deliver important documents and messages. In the modern era, courier businesses have grown in response to the rise of e-commerce and online shopping, with companies like Amazon and FedEx leading the way in the industry.

Legal Requirements

When starting a courier business in the UK, it's important to understand the legal requirements and regulations for operating a delivery service. This includes registering your business with Companies House, obtaining insurance, and applying for any necessary licenses and permits.
