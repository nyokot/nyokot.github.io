What is Dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a business model where the seller does not hold inventory or stock of the products they sell. Instead, the seller lists products on their website or marketplace and when a customer places an order, the seller purchases the product from a supplier who ships it directly to the customer.

The History and Myth of Dropshipping in the UK

Dropshipping has been around for decades, but it has become more popular in recent years due to the rise of ecommerce. Despite its popularity, there are still many myths surrounding dropshipping. Some people believe that dropshipping is a get-rich-quick scheme, while others think that it's too complicated to be profitable. In reality, dropshipping can be a lucrative business model if done correctly.

The Hidden Secret of Dropshipping in the UK
