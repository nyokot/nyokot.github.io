E Commerce Business Models in India PPT - Advantages and Disadvantages

The advantages and disadvantages of each E Commerce Business Model in India PPT are:

  • Inventory Model - Advantages: Complete control over inventory, better margins. Disadvantages: High investment, inventory management.
  • Dropshipping - Advantages: Low investment, no inventory management. Disadvantages: Limited control over inventory, lower margins.
  • Marketplace - Advantages: Large customer base, low investment. Disadvantages: High competition, lower margins.
  • Subscription Model - Advantages: Predictable revenue, higher customer loyalty. Disadvantages: Limited customer base, high churn rate.
