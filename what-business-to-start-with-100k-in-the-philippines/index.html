Recommendation for Starting a Business with 100k in the Philippines

If you're looking for a profitable business with 100k, we recommend investing in a food cart business. Food carts are popular in the Philippines and have a high demand. Starting a food cart business with 100k in the Philippines is relatively easy. Here are the steps:

  2. Find a location with high foot traffic
  3. Buy or rent a food cart
  4. Buy necessary equipment and supplies
  5. Register your business
  6. Market your business
