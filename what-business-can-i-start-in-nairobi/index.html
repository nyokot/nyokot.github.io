This inspired me to start a healthy food delivery service, which has since grown into a successful business.

If you're interested in starting a food and beverage business in Nairobi, there are a few things to keep in mind. Question and Answer

Q: What business can I start in Nairobi with low capital?

A: To find customers for your business in Nairobi, you can use social media, online marketplaces, and local advertising. You can also attend networking events and join industry associations to connect with other professionals in your field.

A: To start a business in Nairobi, you'll need to register your company with the government, obtain any necessary permits and licenses, and comply with local regulations. You'll also need to pay taxes and maintain accurate financial records.
