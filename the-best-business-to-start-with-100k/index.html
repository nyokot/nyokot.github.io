The Best Business to Start with 100k: Real Estate Investing

Real estate investing is one of the best businesses to start with 100k. It has low startup costs and a high potential for profit. With the right strategy, you can turn a small investment into a large return.

When I first started investing in real estate, I was hesitant to put my money into something that seemed so risky. But after doing my research and working with a mentor, I realized that real estate investing can be a smart and lucrative business.

Real estate investing involves buying, renting, or selling properties for profit. You can start small with a single property and grow your portfolio over time. The key is to find properties that are undervalued and have the potential to increase in value over time.
