Home-based Online Business

One of the most popular business ideas for students in Pakistan in 2021 is starting a home-based online business. With the rise of e-commerce and social media, it has become easier than ever to start an online business from the comfort of your own home. Some popular home-based online businesses in Pakistan include:

  • Dropshipping
  • Freelancing
  • Graphic Design
  • Content Writing
  • Affiliate Marketing

Personally, I have experience with dropshipping and it has been a great way to earn extra income. Dropshipping is a business model where you sell products without actually holding inventory. You simply create an online store, list products from a supplier, and when a customer places an order, you forward the order to the supplier who ships the product directly to the customer.
