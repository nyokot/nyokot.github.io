What is the Ghana Club 100?

The Ghana Club 100 is an annual ranking of the top 100 companies in Ghana. It is organized by the Ghana Investment Promotion Centre (GIPC) and serves as a benchmark for success in the country. The rankings take into account a variety of factors, including revenue, profit, growth, and corporate social responsibility.
