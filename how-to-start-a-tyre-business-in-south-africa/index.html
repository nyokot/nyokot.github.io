What is a Tyre Business in South Africa?

A tyre business in South Africa is a company that sells tyres to individuals and businesses. This can include everything from car tyres to truck tyres, and even specialised tyres for mining and construction vehicles. Tyre businesses may also offer services such as tyre fitting, balancing, and repairs.

In South Africa, the tyre industry is highly competitive, with many established players and a growing number of new entrants. However, there is still room for new businesses to succeed, especially if they can differentiate themselves and offer unique value to customers.
