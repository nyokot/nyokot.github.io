What is the FSSAI License for E-commerce Business in India?

The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) is an autonomous body that regulates food safety and hygiene in India. The FSSAI license is a legal requirement for all food businesses, including e-commerce businesses that sell food products. It ensures that the food products sold are safe and of high quality.

To obtain an FSSAI license, e-commerce businesses must comply with the regulations set forth by the FSSAI. These regulations include maintaining proper hygiene and sanitation, ensuring the safety of food products, and following labeling requirements.
