How Much Do Commercial Cleaners Charge Per Hour

Written by Brian May 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Table of Contents

Are you wondering how much commercial cleaners charge per hour? Whether you are a business owner or someone looking for cleaning services, it's important to know the average cost of hiring commercial cleaners.

Many people think that commercial cleaning services are expensive, but the truth is that the cost can vary depending on several factors such as the size of the space, the type of cleaning required, and the location of the business.

The average cost of hiring commercial cleaners can range from $20 to $50 per hour. However, this can go up for larger businesses or for specialized cleaning services such as carpet or window cleaning. It's important to get an estimate from the cleaning company to ensure that you are getting a fair price for the services provided.

Personal Experience with Commercial Cleaners

I recently hired a commercial cleaning company for my small business and was pleasantly surprised by the price. We have a small office space and the cleaning company charged us $25 per hour for weekly cleaning services. The cleaning crew did an excellent job and I felt that the price was fair for the quality of service provided.

What is Commercial Cleaning?

Commercial cleaning refers to cleaning services provided for businesses such as offices, retail spaces, and warehouses. These services can include daily or weekly cleaning of floors, bathrooms, and common areas as well as specialized services like carpet cleaning or window washing. Commercial cleaning companies use specialized equipment and cleaning products to ensure that businesses are kept clean and sanitary.

The History and Myth of Commercial Cleaning Prices

There is a common myth that commercial cleaning services are extremely expensive, but this is not always the case. In fact, the cost of commercial cleaning has decreased over the years due to advancements in technology and equipment. Additionally, many cleaning companies offer competitive pricing to attract new customers.

The Hidden Secret of Commercial Cleaning Prices

The key to getting a good price for commercial cleaning services is to shop around and get quotes from multiple companies. It's also important to communicate your specific cleaning needs to ensure that the company is providing an accurate estimate. Many cleaning companies offer discounts for long-term contracts or for referring new customers.

Recommendations for Commercial Cleaning Prices

If you are looking to hire a commercial cleaning company, it's important to do your research and choose a reputable company with good reviews. You should also ask for references and verify that the company is insured and licensed. It's also a good idea to get multiple quotes so that you can compare pricing and services.

More Information on Commercial Cleaning Prices

If you want to learn more about commercial cleaning prices, there are several resources available online. You can find articles and blogs about the topic as well as forums where business owners discuss their experiences with cleaning companies.

Tips for Negotiating Commercial Cleaning Prices

If you are looking to negotiate commercial cleaning prices, there are a few tips that can help. First, be prepared to walk away if the price is too high. You should also be willing to compromise on certain services to get a better price. Finally, don't be afraid to ask for discounts or special offers. Many cleaning companies are willing to work with customers to provide a fair price.

FAQs about Commercial Cleaning Prices

1. What factors affect the cost of commercial cleaning?
Answer: The size of the space, the type of cleaning required, and the location of the business can all affect the cost of commercial cleaning.

2. Is it possible to negotiate commercial cleaning prices?
Answer: Yes, it is possible to negotiate commercial cleaning prices. It's important to be prepared and to communicate your specific cleaning needs to ensure that you are getting an accurate estimate.

3. How do I choose a reputable commercial cleaning company?
Answer: You should do your research and choose a company with good reviews and references. It's also important to verify that the company is insured and licensed.

4. Is it cheaper to hire an individual cleaner versus a commercial cleaning company?
Answer: It may be cheaper to hire an individual cleaner, but you may not get the same level of service or reliability as you would with a commercial cleaning company.

Conclusion of Commercial Cleaning Prices

Commercial cleaning prices can vary depending on several factors, but the average cost is between $20 and $50 per hour. It's important to get an estimate from multiple companies and to choose a reputable cleaning company with good reviews and references. By doing your research and negotiating pricing, you can ensure that you are getting a fair price for the services provided.
