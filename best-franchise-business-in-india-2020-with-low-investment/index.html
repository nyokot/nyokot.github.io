What Is Best Franchise Business in India 2020 with Low Investment?

A franchise business is a business model in which a franchisor grants the right to use its brand, products, and services to a franchisee in exchange for an initial fee and ongoing royalties. Franchise businesses are a popular choice for entrepreneurs because they offer a proven business model and support system.

Franchise businesses in India with low investment options are those that require a lower initial investment fee and have lower ongoing royalties. These businesses are ideal for entrepreneurs who want to start a business but have limited funds.

History and Myth of Best Franchise Business in India 2020 with Low Investment

The franchise business model has been around for centuries, with the first known franchisor being the Singer Sewing Machine Company in the 1850s. Since then, franchises have become a popular business model across the globe.

There is a common myth that franchise businesses are only for big corporations and require a significant initial investment. However, there are many franchise opportunities in India that require a low investment and have proven success rates.

The Hidden Secret of Best Franchise Business in India 2020 with Low Investment

The hidden secret to running a successful franchise business in India with a low investment is research and planning. What is the initial investment required for a low investment franchise business in India?

The initial investment required for a low investment franchise business in India varies depending on the franchise opportunity. However, most low investment franchises require an initial investment of between INR 5 lakhs to INR 20 lakhs.

What are the ongoing royalties for a low investment franchise business in India?

The ongoing royalties for a low investment franchise business in India typically range from 3% to 10% of gross sales.
