What is a Kumon Franchise?

Kumon is a worldwide educational organization that helps children succeed academically through its unique approach to learning. The Kumon Method focuses on building strong foundational skills in math and reading through individualized instruction. Kumon has been in business for over 60 years and has over 4 million students enrolled worldwide.

History and Myth of Kumon Franchise

Kumon was founded in 1958 by Toru Kumon, a Japanese math teacher who wanted to help his son improve his math skills. The Kumon Method quickly gained popularity in Japan and expanded globally, with the first Kumon center opening in the United States in 1974.

The Hidden Secret of Kumon Franchise

One of the hidden secrets of the Kumon franchise is the extensive training and support provided to franchisees. Kumon provides initial training, ongoing support, and access to a vast network of resources to help franchisees succeed. Kumon also offers marketing and advertising support, which is crucial for new franchisees to get their business off the ground.
