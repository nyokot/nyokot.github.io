With a Clean Eatz franchise, there are several costs to consider, including the franchise fee, equipment, inventory, and real estate.

The initial investment for a Clean Eatz franchise ranges from $181,850 to $349,900. This includes a franchise fee of $35,000, equipment costs of $50,000 to $75,000, and an estimated $20,000 to $50,000 for inventory and real estate expenses.
