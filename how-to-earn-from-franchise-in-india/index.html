The simplest way to earn from franchise in India is by partnering with an established brand and replicating their business model. By paying a franchise fee, you can gain access to the brand's trademark, products, services, and marketing support. You can then set up your own franchise outlet and start earning profits from sales. Depending on the franchise agreement, you may also be required to pay royalties, purchase supplies from the franchisor, and follow their guidelines for operations.
