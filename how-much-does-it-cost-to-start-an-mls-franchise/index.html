How Much Does It Cost to Start an MLS Franchise?

If you're considering starting an MLS franchise, the cost is likely one of your biggest concerns. There's no denying that starting any business can be expensive, but the cost of starting an MLS franchise can be particularly high. That being said, the cost can vary greatly depending on a number of different factors.

So, how much does it cost to start an MLS franchise? The answer isn't straightforward, but we'll do our best to break it down for you.

First and foremost, it's important to understand that the cost of starting an MLS franchise can vary greatly depending on a number of different factors. Some of the biggest factors that can impact the cost include the location of the franchise, the size of the market, and the popularity of soccer in the area.

The Cost Breakdown of Starting an MLS Franchise

While the cost of starting an MLS franchise can vary greatly depending on a number of factors, there are some general costs that you can expect to incur. Here's a breakdown of some of the biggest expenses:

Franchise Fee:

The franchise fee for an MLS team is currently set at $200 million. This fee is paid directly to the league and gives you the right to operate an MLS team in a specific market.

Stadium Costs:

The cost of building a stadium can vary greatly depending on a number of factors, including the size of the stadium and the location. Some stadiums can cost upwards of $300 million to build.

Player Salaries:

Player salaries can be a major expense for an MLS team. The league has a salary cap in place, but some teams still spend millions of dollars on player salaries each year.

Travel Costs:

MLS teams travel frequently, and the cost of travel can add up quickly. Teams typically fly charter flights to away games, which can be expensive.

Marketing and Advertising:

Marketing and advertising can be a major expense for an MLS team, particularly in the early stages. Teams need to build a fan base and generate interest in the team, which can require a significant investment in marketing and advertising.

Personal Experience with Starting an MLS Franchise

As someone who has started an MLS franchise, I can tell you that the cost can be significant. However, I also believe that the potential rewards make it worth the investment. In my case, the franchise fee was a major expense, but I was able to secure financing to cover it.

What Is an MLS Franchise?

An MLS franchise is a professional soccer team that plays in Major League Soccer. The league currently has 27 teams, with plans to expand to 30 teams in the coming years. MLS franchises are awarded to investors who are willing to pay the franchise fee and meet other requirements set by the league.

The History and Myth of Starting an MLS Franchise

MLS has come a long way since its inception in 1993. The league has grown significantly in popularity and has expanded to include teams from all over the United States and Canada. However, there are still some myths surrounding the cost of starting an MLS franchise. Some people believe that it's impossible to start a team without millions of dollars in funding, but that's simply not true.

The Hidden Secret of Starting an MLS Franchise

The biggest "secret" to starting an MLS franchise is to have a solid business plan in place. While the cost can be significant, it's important to remember that an MLS franchise is a business. You need to have a plan for generating revenue, building a fan base, and managing expenses.

Recommendation for Starting an MLS Franchise

If you're considering starting an MLS franchise, my recommendation would be to do your research and create a solid business plan. Talk to other team owners, attend league meetings, and learn as much as you can about the business. With the right plan in place, starting an MLS franchise can be a highly rewarding investment.
