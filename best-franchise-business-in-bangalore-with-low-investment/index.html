What is Best Franchise Business in Bangalore with Low Investment?

A franchise business is a type of business where an individual (franchisee) enters into an agreement with a company (franchisor) to use their brand name, products and services, and operating system for a fee. The franchisor provides the franchisee with training and support to ensure the success of the business.

Low investment franchise businesses are those that require minimal initial investment, making them an attractive option for those who want to start a business but do not have a lot of capital.

History and Myth of Best Franchise Business in Bangalore with Low Investment

The concept of franchising dates back to the Middle Ages when landowners would grant certain rights to others in exchange for a fee. The modern-day concept of franchising began in the US in the 1850s, with the opening of Coca-Cola and Singer Sewing Machine franchise businesses.

There is a common myth that franchise businesses are only for those who have a lot of money to invest. However, there are many low investment franchise business opportunities available, making it accessible to a wider range of people.

Understanding the Franchise Agreement

Before investing in a franchise business, it is important to understand the franchise agreement. This agreement outlines the terms and conditions of the franchise, including the franchise fee, royalty fees, and obligations of both the franchisor and franchisee.
