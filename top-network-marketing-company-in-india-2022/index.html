What is Multi-Level Marketing?

Multi-level marketing (MLM) is a business model that allows members to earn commissions not only from their own sales but also from the sales made by their downline members. MLM is often used interchangeably with network marketing, but there is a subtle difference between the two.

Network marketing is a subset of MLM and focuses more on building a network of distributors to promote and sell products. MLM, on the other hand, can refer to any business model that involves multiple levels of commission-based sales.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the difference between network marketing and direct selling?

A. Network marketing is a subset of direct selling that focuses on building a network of distributors to promote and sell products. Direct selling, on the other hand, refers to any business model that involves selling products directly to customers through a network of independent distributors.

Q. Is network marketing a pyramid scheme?

A. No, network marketing is not a pyramid scheme. Pyramid schemes are illegal and involve making money solely by recruiting new members into the scheme, without any actual products or services being sold. Network marketing, on the other hand, involves selling products and earning commissions from those sales, as well as from the sales made by your downline members.

Q. How much can I earn with a top network marketing company in India?

A. Your earnings with a top network marketing company in India will depend on your sales and the sales made by your downline members. The more you sell and the larger your team, the higher your earning potential. However, it is important to note that network marketing is not a get-rich-quick scheme and requires hard work and dedication to succeed.

Q. Can I join a top network marketing company in India part-time?
