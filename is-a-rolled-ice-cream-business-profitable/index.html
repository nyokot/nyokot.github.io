What Is a Rolled Ice Cream Business?

Rolled ice cream is a unique dessert trend that originated in Thailand and has since spread around the world. It is made by pouring a liquid ice cream base onto a cold surface, where it is mixed with toppings and rolled into thin, cylindrical shapes. This dessert is typically served in a cup and can be customized with a wide range of flavors and toppings.

A rolled ice cream business involves creating and selling this delicious dessert to customers. This can be done through a brick-and-mortar store, a food truck, or an online ordering system. To start a rolled ice cream business, you'll need to invest in equipment, ingredients, and marketing efforts.
