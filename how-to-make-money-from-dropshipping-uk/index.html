What is Dropshipping in the UK?

Dropshipping is a business model that allows you to sell products without holding inventory. Instead of purchasing products upfront and storing them in a warehouse, you work with a supplier who ships products directly to your customers. This allows you to focus on marketing and sales, rather than logistics and fulfillment.
