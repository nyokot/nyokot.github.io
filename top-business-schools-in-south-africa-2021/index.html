Question and Answer

What is the average cost of attending a top business school in South Africa?

The cost of attending a top business school in South Africa can vary depending on the program and the institution. However, you can expect to pay anywhere from R200,000 to R500,000 for a full-time MBA program.

What types of programs are offered by top business schools in South Africa?

Top business schools in South Africa offer a wide range of programs, including full-time MBA programs, executive MBA programs, specialized master's programs, and executive education programs.

What are the admission requirements for top business schools in South Africa?

The admission requirements for top business schools in South Africa can vary depending on the institution and the program. Generally, however, you will need to have a bachelor's degree or equivalent, as well as relevant work experience and strong academic credentials. Some programs may also require you to take the GMAT or GRE.

What is the typical class size at top business schools in South Africa?

The typical class size at top business schools in South Africa can vary depending on the program and the institution. However, you can expect to be part of a relatively small and close-knit community of students, with class sizes ranging from 30 to 100 students.
