What is Pizza Pizza?

Founded in 1967 in Toronto, Ontario, Pizza Pizza is a Canadian pizza franchise that has grown to become one of the largest pizza chains in the country. They offer a wide variety of pizzas, including vegetarian and halal options, as well as other menu items such as wings, pasta, and salads.

History and Myth of Pizza Pizza

Legend has it that the founder of Pizza Pizza, Michael Overs, came up with the name while watching a Toronto Maple Leafs hockey game. When the Leafs scored a goal, the announcer would shout "Pizza Pizza!" to signal that fans could get a free slice of pizza. Overs saw an opportunity and decided to name his pizza franchise after the iconic phrase.

Hidden Secret of Pizza Pizza

One of the best-kept secrets of Pizza Pizza is their "Walk-In Special" deal. If you walk into any Pizza Pizza location and ask for the Walk-In Special, you can get a medium cheese or pepperoni pizza for only $5.99. This is a great deal for those looking for a quick and affordable pizza option.

Recommendation of Pizza Pizza

What Makes Pizza Pizza the Best?

Tips for Ordering Pizza Pizza

What Makes Pizza Pizza Unique?

Fun Facts About Pizza Pizza
