What are Franchises?

A franchise is essentially a business model in which an individual (the franchisee) purchases the right to use a company's (the franchisor's) name, brand, and business model. In exchange, the franchisee pays an initial franchise fee and ongoing royalties to the franchisor. The franchisor provides training, support, and assistance in various areas of the business, such as marketing, operations, and management.

History and Myths of Franchises

The concept of franchising dates back to the Middle Ages, when landowners would lease their land to tenants in exchange for a share of their profits. However, the modern franchise model as we know it today began in the United States in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Some myths about franchises include that they are only for big corporations or that they are too expensive for small business owners. In reality, there are many affordable franchise options available for entrepreneurs of all sizes.

Q: How much does it cost to start a franchise in India?
A: The cost of starting a franchise in India can vary widely depending on the industry, the franchise model, and other factors. Some franchises require an initial investment of several lakhs or crores of rupees, while others may only require a few lakhs.

Q: What are the benefits of franchising in India?
A: Franchising in India can provide numerous benefits, such as a proven business model, established brand recognition, and support and guidance from the franchisor. Additionally, franchising can be less risky than starting a business from scratch.

Q: Can I franchise a business that I have already started?
A: Yes, it is possible to franchise a business that you have already started. However, you will need to have a proven business model and a solid understanding of the industry and market conditions.

Q: Can I franchise a business that is not based in India?
